Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Shopping List Saver extension? +

The Shopping List Saver extension is a tool that helps you create, organize, and share your shopping lists, making your shopping experience more efficient and stress-free.

How do I install the extension? +

You can install the extension from the Chrome Web Store by clicking the "Download Now" button on our website and following the installation instructions.

Is the Shopping List Saver extension free? +

Yes, the Shopping List Saver extension is completely free to use.

Can I sync my shopping lists across multiple devices? +

Yes, you can sync your shopping lists across multiple devices. Simply log in with the same account on all your devices to access your lists anywhere, anytime.

How do I share my shopping list with others? +

You can share your shopping list with others by clicking the "Share" button within the extension and sending the generated link to your family or friends.

Can I organize items by categories? +

Yes, you can organize items by categories to make your shopping experience more streamlined and efficient.
